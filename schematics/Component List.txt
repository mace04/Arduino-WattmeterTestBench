Arduino Wattmeter
====================
Box enclosure
3 male small connectors (ESC motor)
T connector female (ESC battery)
3 pin male futuba connector (ESC signal)
T connector male (battery)
3 cables to test motors
3 small female connectors (test motors)
4 pin male futaba connector (weight sensor)

potentiometer (throttle)
LCD screen (64x4)
push buttons x2 (page, reset)
